üìñ History & Spiritual Knowledge

Tirth Purohits have been the custodians of Sanatan Dharma for generations. They keep detailed family records, perform life-changing rituals, and guide devotees in dharmik journeys. Their presence is central at every tirth (pilgrimage).

Haridwar ‚Äî one of the seven holiest places in Hinduism ‚Äî is where the Ganga meets the Himalayas. It is believed that gods have performed penance here, and Lord Vishnu left his footprint on the ghats.

Why is Pind Daan Important?

Pind Daan is a sacred offering to departed souls. It is believed to grant peace and moksha to ancestors. The ritual involves offering pind (rice balls) with mantra chanting to liberate the souls and bless future generations.

Why Asthi Visarjan at Haridwar?

Haridwar is where the ashes of the deceased are immersed in the Ganga to aid their soul's journey to heaven. It is believed the Ganga at Har Ki Pauri carries the soul towards liberation.

Significance of Narayani Pooja

Narayani Pooja is performed for blessings from Maa Durga. It brings peace, family harmony, and removes negative energies. Ideal for overcoming difficulties and attracting divine grace.

Why do we do Tri Pindi Shradh?

Tri Pindi Shradh is performed for the last three generations of ancestors. It helps unfulfilled souls get peace and blesses the current and future generations with health, wealth, and happiness.
