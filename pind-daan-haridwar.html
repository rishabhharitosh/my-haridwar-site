📿 Book Pind Daan in Haridwar

Looking to book Pind Daan in Haridwar? It is a sacred Hindu ritual to pay homage to ancestors and help their souls attain moksha. Our experienced Tirth Purohits guide the complete ceremony as per Vedic traditions at Har Ki Pauri.

📌 What is Pind?

Pind are rice balls made of rice flour, til (black sesame seeds), barley, and ghee. They symbolize nourishment to ancestral souls and are offered during the ritual.

🙏 Why is Pind Daan Performed?

  • To help ancestors attain moksha (liberation).
  • To fulfill the spiritual duty (pitru rin) of the family.
  • To remove ancestral dosha and gain blessings.
  • To bring peace and prosperity in one’s home.

📍 Why Haridwar for Pind Daan?

Haridwar is one of the most sacred tirthas where the holy river Ganga flows. Performing Pind Daan at Har Ki Pauri is believed to guide souls towards salvation. Our purohits ensure all rituals are done with proper samagri and vidhi.

🕉️ How is Pind Daan Performed?

  1. Purification with Ganga Jal
  2. Preparation of pinds and offering with mantras
  3. Sankalp and tilanjali for ancestors
  4. Offering pinds at the banks of the Ganga
  5. Feeding Brahmins or cows (optional)

🧺 Samagri Required

  • Rice flour, til (black sesame), ghee
  • Kusha grass, diya, incense
  • Ganga Jal, flowers, puja thali
  • Pitru name or gotra (if available)

⏳ Time & Booking

Duration: 30 to 45 mins
Best Time: Early morning (5 AM – 8 AM)
